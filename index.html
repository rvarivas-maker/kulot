about us

Peacefulness and Adventure - This is what makes Anya's Burobanglid extra special. It is located at Brgy. Callan, Sebaste, Antique where you can enjoy different activities that they offer. Some of those activities are swimming, nature tripping and sight seeing. They have two swimming pools intended for kids and adults. To those who love to explore , you could also let yourself enjoy and blend with nature for they have giant birds' nests, spider web, a giant coconut, swings, and a wishing well. They also accept catering services and serve as a venue for some special events. If you'd love to stay overnight, the place also offers cottages and air-conditioned rooms. If you're fond of adventure, Anya's Burobanglid must be on your bucket list!
